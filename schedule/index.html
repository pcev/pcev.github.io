ESSAY WRITING

1. Separate competitions will be there in Malayalam and English
2. Maximum time allotted is 60 minutes

RANGOLI

1. Duration will not be more than 1 hour.
2. Participants should bring their own material.
3. This art is known differently in various regions such as Mandna, Alpana, Alekhan, Kolam, Rangoli, etc. For this the medium and form for expression can be free hand pictorial and descriptive.
4. Only one medium should be used Poster Colours or Flower Petals or Saw- dust Without pasting, gilt powder is not permitted.
5. The Participants should prepare a Rangoli within the space provided by the organizers.
6. A team consist of 2 members
7. Maximum 2 teams will be allowed from each department

STORY WRITING

1. Separate competitions will be there in Malayalam and English
2. Maximum time allotted is 60 minutes

POEM WRITING

1. Separate competitions will be there in Malayalam and English
2. Maximum time allotted is 60 minutes

ELOCUTION

1. Maximum time allowed is 5 minutes
2. All the other rules are same as above

FILM REVIEW

1. Total time of competition is 90 minutes.
2. A movie will be played by program committee
3. The participants have to write a review about the same movie in 60 minutes
4. Uses of any electronic devices such as mobile phones/tablet, pcs are strictly prohibited

QUIZ

1. Quiz will be conducted by Quiz master.
2. Pattern and method of Quiz will be disclosed by the quiz master at the event
3. Winners will be determined based on the total points secured by the teams
4. 4 teams from each department (2 members each)

OIL PAINTING

1. Subject will be given on the spot
2. Duration will not be more than 1 hours
3. Painting can be done in oil colors.
4. Candidate should bring their own material like brushes, paints etc. Only the paper/sheet will be provided by the host institute.

PHOTOGRAPHY

1. Maximum time allowed is 20 minutes
2. Except mobile cameras all other cameras can be used (Including SLR & Digital Cameras)
3. 4 members from 1 department can participate
4. Individual participation

POSTER DESIGN

1. subject / topic / theme given by the In- charge of the competition on the spot.
2. Duration will not be more than 1 hours
3. Participants should bring their own material.

COLLAGE

1. Item will be conducted on the spot on the given topic.
2. Duration will not be more than 30 minutes.

CLAY MODELING

1. event will be conducted on the spot.
2. Duration will not be more than 3 hours.
3. Topics/ size and other specific rules will be announced on the spot.
4. Clay will be provided by the host department.

PENCIL DRAWING

1. Event will be conducted on the spot on the given subject/idea
2. Duration should not be more than 1 hour
3. Participants should bring their own materials

CARICATURE

1. Event will be conducted on the spot on the given subject/idea
2. Duration should not be more than 1 hour
3. Participants should bring their own materials

MEHENDI

1. 4 teams from each department (2 members each)
2. Participants have to carry their own materials
3. Maximum time allowed is 1 hour

CARTOON DRAWING

1. Event will be conducted on the spot on the given subject/ idea.
2. Duration will not be more than 1 hour.
3. Participants should bring their own material

JAM

1. Event will be conducted on the spot on the given idea/subject
2. preparation time is 1 minute
3. The speech should not exceed 1 minute

FIRELESS COOKING

1. 2 teams from each department (3 members each)
2. Duration will be not more than 1 hour
3. Usage of heat not allowed
4. Materials will be provided
